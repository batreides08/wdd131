San Jose, Costa Rica

Costa Rica is a vibrant Central American nation celebrated for its rich biodiversity, peaceful society, and environmental leadership. With over 25% of its land protected as national parks and reserves, it's a global model for conservation. The country abolished its military in 1948, choosing to invest in education and healthcare, and today boasts a literacy rate of over 97%. Nearly 99% of its electricity comes from renewable sources, primarily hydro, wind, and geothermal energy.
