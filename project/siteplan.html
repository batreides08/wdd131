Tanager Website Plan

1. Site Name

TanagerCR.com — This site will serve as a hub for foreigners interested in buying or renting property in Costa Rica. “Tanager” reflects the vibrant local bird species and symbolizes the connection between nature and lifestyle. The name is short, memorable, and brandable. A domain such as tanagercr.com or livetanager.com would be ideal for branding and recognition.

2. Site Purpose

The site will guide foreigners in navigating Costa Rica’s real estate market. It will provide legal tips, area highlights, and showcase the value of using the Tanager platform to make safe and informed property decisions. It will also connect users with trusted agents and services.

3. Scenarios

  • What legal steps do I need to take to purchase property as a foreigner in Costa Rica?
  • Which area is better suited for a family lifestyle — Escazú or Santa Teresa?
  • How can I know that the listing I’m seeing is trustworthy or still available?

4. Color Schema

  • Forest Green (#2a6f62): Used for headings, buttons, and accents — evokes nature and trust.
  • Sky Blue (#a3d5f7): Used for backgrounds and highlights — conveys clarity and peace of mind.
  • Neutral White (#ffffff) and Light Gray (#f2f5f7): For backgrounds and text contrast.

5. Typography

  • Headings: 'Playfair Display', serif – for elegance and visual hierarchy.
  • Body text: 'Segoe UI', sans-serif – for readability and a modern feel.
  • Special highlights: Italic or bold serif for emphasis in quotes or testimonials.
