Buying Legally as a Foreigner

Foreigners have the same property rights as Costa Ricans. It's recommended to hire a local attorney to guide you through due diligence, title verification, and property registration.

Rental Laws

Rental contracts typically last 3 years unless stated otherwise. Always request a written contract with clauses reviewed by your lawyer.
